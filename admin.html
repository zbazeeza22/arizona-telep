Assessment Quiz — Validated Screening Tools

PHQ-9 (Depression)

9-item Patient Health Questionnaire. Gold standard for depression screening. Score 0–27, cutoff ≥10 for moderate depression.

GAD-7 (Anxiety)

7-item Generalized Anxiety Disorder scale. Score 0–21, cutoff ≥10 for moderate anxiety. Takes 2 minutes.

ASRS v1.1 (ADHD)

Adult ADHD Self-Report Scale. 6-item screener developed by WHO. High sensitivity for adult ADHD.

ISI (Insomnia)

Insomnia Severity Index. 7-item questionnaire. Score 0–28, cutoff ≥15 for moderate insomnia.
