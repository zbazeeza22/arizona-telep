Assessment Quiz — Validated Screening Tools

PHQ-9 (Depression)

9-item Patient Health Questionnaire. Gold standard for depression screening. Score 0–27, cutoff ≥10 for moderate depression.

GAD-7 (Anxiety)

7-item Generalized Anxiety Disorder scale. Score 0–21, cutoff ≥10 for moderate anxiety. Takes 2 minutes.

ASRS v1.1 (ADHD)

Adult ADHD Self-Report Scale. 6-item screener developed by WHO. High sensitivity for adult ADHD.

ISI (Insomnia)

Insomnia Severity Index. 7-item questionnaire. Score 0–28, cutoff ≥15 for moderate insomnia.

Compliance Center — Legal & Regulatory

Critical compliance requirements for Arizona telepsychiatry operations.

Non-Negotiable

Arizona Telehealth Laws

  • ✓ Telehealth Parity: Insurance must cover telehealth same as in-person (A.R.S. § 20-841.09)
  • ✓ Audio-Only Permitted: For behavioral health services (expands rural reach significantly)
  • ! Schedule II Exception: MUST use audio-visual for stimulant prescribing (audio-only not allowed)
  • ! CSPMP Registration: Register with AZ Controlled Substances Prescription Monitoring Program before prescribing
  • ✓ Out-of-State Providers: Must register with AZ boards (can use OpenLoop's credentialing)

Federal Requirements

DEA Telehealth Extension

Extended through Dec 31, 2026. Ryan Haight Act waiver still active. MONITOR for permanent rulemaking — if it expires, all Schedule II telehealth prescribing may require in-person visit first.

HIPAA — Zero Tolerance

NEVER share PHI with ad platforms. Cerebral was fined $7M for sharing patient data with LinkedIn, Snapchat, and TikTok via tracking pixels. Use server-side conversion tracking only.

FTC Advertising Rules

No fake testimonials, no AI deepfakes, proper endorsement disclosures. FTC Health Claims Act applies to all health-related advertising claims.

ADS for Mental Health Act (Dec 2025)

New reporting requirements for mental health digital advertising. Requires disclosure of targeting criteria and ad spend to FTC. Applies to telehealth advertisers.

Safe Ad Messaging

  • "Learn about ADHD management options"
  • "Schedule a mental health evaluation"

  • "Evidence-based psychiatric treatment online"

Prohibited Ad Messaging

  • "Cure your depression" (no cure claims)

LegitScript Certification Required

Meta and Google require LegitScript certification for healthcare advertising. Apply at legitscript.com — approval takes 2–4 weeks. Cost: ~$1,000–2,000/yr. Without this, your health ads will be rejected or your account suspended.
