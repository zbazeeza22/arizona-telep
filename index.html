Treatment, step by step

A structured process designed for adults—assessment first, then a plan that fits your goals.

Appointment 1

Intake & assessment

Meet your provider, review symptoms and history, and start an evidence‑based evaluation.

  • • 30‑minute video visit
  • • Clear next steps
  • • Upfront self‑pay pricing

Appointment 2

Annual wellness check

A recent wellness exam helps ensure your full health picture is considered before starting a treatment plan.

  • • Often covered by insurance
  • • Can be completed virtually
  • • Documentation accepted if recent

  • • Clear expectations
  • • Follow‑ups scheduled online

  • • Secure messaging
  • • Continuous improvement

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

Our initial psychiatric evaluation is $179 for self-pay patients, with follow-up appointments at $120. If you have insurance, your typical out-of-pocket cost ranges from $0-$40 depending on your plan. We accept most major insurance providers in Arizona.

We specialize in ADHD, anxiety disorders, depression, insomnia, PTSD, and other common psychiatric conditions in adults. Our board-certified providers create individualized treatment plans based on your specific symptoms and goals.

All appointments are conducted via secure HIPAA-compliant video. You’ll connect with your provider from the comfort of your home using any device with a camera. No downloads or special software required.

Most new patients are seen within the same week. After your initial evaluation, follow-up appointments are scheduled monthly or as needed based on your treatment plan.
