What Is Telepsychiatry?

Telepsychiatry is the delivery of psychiatric care through secure video conferencing. It allows you to receive a full evaluation, diagnosis, medication management, and ongoing follow-up care from a board-certified psychiatric provider — without visiting a physical office.

How Does Online Psychiatry Work?

Online psychiatry works the same as a traditional in-office visit, except you connect with your provider from home through a secure, HIPAA-compliant video platform. Your provider conducts a comprehensive psychiatric evaluation, discusses your symptoms and medical history, reviews treatment options, and — when appropriate — prescribes medication electronically to your preferred pharmacy. Research consistently shows that telepsychiatry produces outcomes equivalent to in-person psychiatric care.

Insurance & Pricing

We believe cost should never be a barrier to mental health care. Arizona law requires insurers to cover telepsychiatry at the same rate as in-person visits.

Is Telepsychiatry Covered by Insurance in Arizona?

Yes. Arizona’s telehealth parity law (A.R.S. §20-841.09) requires health insurance companies to cover telehealth services, including telepsychiatry, at the same rate as equivalent in-person services. This means your copay for a telepsychiatry visit should be the same as an office visit with a psychiatrist.
